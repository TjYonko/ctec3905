I've developed an app called FlixMovies, designed to cater to movie enthusiasts seeking personalized recommendations. Utilizing content-based filtering, FlixMovies analyzes the inherent characteristics of films—such as genre, director, cast, and plot themes—to recommend new movies that match users' individual tastes.
